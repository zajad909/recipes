# Stuffed Peppers

## Ingredients

### Peppers & Rice

- 4 bell peppers
- 2 x 250g pouches cooked tomato rice or golden veggie rice
- 2 tbsp pesto

### Protein & Cheese

- Chicken (amount to taste)
- Cheese for filling (mozzarella, cheddar, or Parmesan work best)
- Extra cheese for topping

## Method

1. **Prepare the Peppers:**
   - Use a small knife to cut the top off each pepper.
   - Carefully scoop out the seeds and white membranes.
   - Place peppers cut-side up on a plate and set aside.

2. **Prepare the Filling:**
   - Mix the two pouches of cooked rice together.
   - Add the pesto and mix well.
   - Cook the chicken and chop into bite-sized pieces.
   - Combine the rice mixture with the cooked chicken and cheese.

3. **Fill the Peppers:**
   - Scoop the rice, chicken, and cheese mixture into each pepper.
   - Top each pepper with extra grated cheese.

4. **Bake:**
   - Place peppers on a baking sheet.
   - Bake at 200°C for 8–10 minutes until the cheese has melted and is golden.

5. **Serve:**
   - Remove from oven and serve immediately while hot.

## Tips

- Make sure the peppers stand upright on the baking sheet by trimming the bottom if needed.
- You can prepare the peppers and filling ahead of time and refrigerate until ready to bake.
- Add extra vegetables like corn or diced tomatoes to the rice mixture for more texture.
