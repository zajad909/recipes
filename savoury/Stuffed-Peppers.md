Stuffed peppers

Ingredients

- 4 bell peppers
- 2 x pouches cooked tomato or golden veggie rice
- 2 tbsp pesto
- Cheese, mozerella, cheddar or Parmesan work best.
- Chicken

Method

- Use a small knife to cut the top out of 4 red peppers, then scoop out the seeds. Sit the peppers on a plate, cut-side up, and set aside.  
- Mix two 250g pouches cooked tomato rice together with 2 tbsp pesto and, fry with some chicken and cheese (optional) 
- Scoop the rice, chicken and cheese mix into the peppers, top with some extra greated cheese and oven cook for 8-10 mins. (200°C)
