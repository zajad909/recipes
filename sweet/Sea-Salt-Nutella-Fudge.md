# Sea Salt Nutella Fudge

## Ingredients

### Base

- 1 x 397g can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup (200g) bittersweet (dark) chocolate chips
- 1 cup (250g) Nutella
- 45ml butter (3 tbsp), salted or unsalted (your choice)

### Topping

- Coarse/flaked sea salt for sprinkling

## Method

1. **Combine Ingredients:**
   - Add the sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, chocolate chips, Nutella, and butter to a glass bowl.

2. **Mix:**
   - Stir the ingredients together until combined.

3. **Slow Cook:**
   - Place the bowl over a pot of gently simmering water (double boiler).
   - Cook on low heat for 10 minutes, stirring constantly.
   - Continue until everything is melted, smooth, and starting to thicken.

4. **Prepare the Dish:**
   - Line a shallow baking dish with baking paper.

5. **Pour the Fudge:**
   - Pour the fudge mixture into the prepared baking dish.
   - Use a spatula to spread evenly.

6. **Add Sea Salt:**
   - Sprinkle coarse or flaked sea salt generously over the top.

7. **Set:**
   - Refrigerate for at least 2–3 hours until the fudge is set and firm.

8. **Serve:**
   - Cut into squares and enjoy!

## Tips

- Use a double boiler to gently melt the ingredients — don't overheat.
- The longer you chill, the firmer the fudge will be.
- The salty-sweet contrast is key — don't be shy with the sea salt topping.
- Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
