Recipe
14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup bittersweet (dark) chocolate chips
1 cup of Nutella
3 TBSP of butter (salted or unsalted your choice)
Coarse/flaked Sea salt to sprinkle on top
Steps 1. Add sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, chocolate chips, Nutella, and butter to a glass bowl 2. Mix together 3. slow cook on low heat for 10 minutes, stirring constantly until everything is melted, smooth, and it starts thickening 4. Line a shallow baking dish with baking paper 5. Pour fudge into baking dish and sprinkle on some sea salt 6. Let sit in the refrigerator for at least 2-3 hours to set up 7. EAT
